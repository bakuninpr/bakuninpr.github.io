Research

Data Science & Statistical Methods

My research focuses on developing scalable computational methods for spatial statistics, with emphasis on uncertainty quantification for massive non-Gaussian datasets.

01

Research Areas

01

Gaussian Process Regression

Scalable algorithms for GP regression including Fixed Rank Kriging, low-rank approximations, and efficient computational methods for large spatial datasets.

02

Non-Gaussian Processes

Fixed Rank Non-Gaussian Process Regression (FRNGPR) methods for κ-lognormal and other non-Gaussian distributions in spatial modeling.

03

Uncertainty Quantification

Optimal prediction and uncertainty quantification methods for massive datasets, with focus on computational efficiency and statistical rigor.

04

Multivariate Spatial Modeling

Efficient algorithms for multivariate spatial processes, coregionalization models, and cross-covariance estimation at scale.
